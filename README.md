## Project Overview
This project is created as part of the Digital Literacy course. It aims to develop essential digital skills such as creating a professional online presence, using digital tools, practicing safe internet usage, and understanding cyber security. The project consists of five tasks that demonstrate practical knowledge and awareness of digital literacy.

---

## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
An infographic was created using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy. It includes topics such as safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette. The design focuses on clarity, simplicity, and visual appeal to make the information easy to understand.

---

## Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
Profiles were created on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms help in building a professional identity, showcasing skills, and connecting with opportunities. This portfolio will be useful for future internships and career growth.

---

## Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
A beginner-level problem was solved on HackerRank to gain basic coding experience. Additionally, a Google Form titled "Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz" was created to test knowledge about digital safety and tools.

## Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Social Media Awareness
Two professional emails were drafted:
- Requesting assignment deadline extension  
- Applying for an internship  

A social media checklist was also created with important Do’s and Don’ts to ensure responsible online behavior.

---

## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
A case study on phishing was created to explain how cybercrime occurs and its impact. A prevention checklist was also prepared to provide practical tips for staying safe online, including UPI safety and reporting cybercrime.
